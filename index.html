🎮 About KeyArena

KeyArena is a fast-paced typing game designed to boost your typing speed and accuracy through engaging micro-stories and daily life hacks.

🚀 Our Mission

To make typing practice fun, effective, and interactive for everyday self-improvement.

✨ Features

  • ⌨️ Real-time typing practice
  • ⏱️ Preset & custom countdown timers
  • 🏆 Instant score tracking
  • 📖 Category-based story progression
  • 📱 Fully responsive layout

🎯 How to Play

  1. Select your favorite category & time duration.
  2. Click Start Game and type displayed words accurately.
  3. Beat the timer and complete the full story!

🛠 Technologies Used

Built with HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript (ES6+).
